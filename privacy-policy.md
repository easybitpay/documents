## 2. What we do (non-custodial services)

EasyBitPay provides:

- A **non-custodial crypto wallet** for end users.
- A **crypto payment gateway** for merchants.

Key points:

- We **do not** store your private keys or seed phrases.
- We **do not** hold your crypto funds or have direct control over your wallets.
- Payments occur directly on blockchain networks between payer and merchant.

Any private keys generated by our Wallet or interfaces are stored **locally on your device** (for example, in application storage or in your own cloud backup, if you choose to use one). We do not have access to these private keys.

---

## 3. Information we do **not** collect

We **do not**:

- Store or have access to your **private keys**.
- Store or have access to your **seed phrases** or recovery phrases.
- Control or move your funds on your behalf.

If you lose your device or seed phrase, we cannot recover your funds for you.

We aim to collect only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve the Services.

### 4.1. Merchant account and contact information

If you use the Services as a merchant or business user, we may collect:

To operate the payment gateway and provide accounting views, we may process:

We do not control blockchain networks and cannot modify or delete on-chain data.

### 4.3. Usage data and log information

When you access our website or apps, we may automatically collect:

- App version and basic diagnostic data

We may use tools such as **Google Analytics** (for usage analytics) and **Sentry** (for error monitoring and performance) or similar services to help us understand and improve the Services.

### 4.4. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Maintain user sessions

You can usually control cookies via your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some features of the site may not work correctly.
